About Me

Diya Parmar

Bachelor of Architecture · Parul Institution, Vadodara

I am an architecture student passionate about shaping spaces that bring people together. My interests lie in urban design, community-focused architecture, and landscape — where I explore how thoughtful design can transform everyday environments into meaningful experiences.

I am particularly inspired by the interaction between people, public spaces, and nature. I enjoy designing environments that encourage connection, movement, and community life — ensuring design decisions are responsive, functional, and socially relevant.
